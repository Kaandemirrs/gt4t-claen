GT4T Kick-Off Meeting: A Promising Start in Pori

Our very first GT4T consortium meeting took place on June 2–3, 2025, at SAMK Pori Campus. This Kick-Off Meeting marked a significant milestone in aligning our shared vision and setting the foundation for successful collaboration. Partners from across Europe gathered to present work packages, discuss the project’s roadmap, and enjoy a warm and inspiring social program in Yyteri and Anttoora. Thank you to everyone who contributed — the journey has officially begun!
